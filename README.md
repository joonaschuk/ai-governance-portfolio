## 🔧 What's here

**EU AI Act Classifier** — [live tool](https://joonaschuk.github.io/ai-governance-portfolio) · [source](./eu-ai-act-classifier/)

An interactive decision tree that works out how the EU AI Act (Regulation (EU) 2024/1689) applies to a given AI system — from prohibited practices and high-risk categories to GPAI obligations, transparency requirements, and minimal risk. Built in React; the logic is a hand-built decision tree, not an AI model. Available in English and Finnish.
